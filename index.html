ABOUT ME

A World Class Beauty Salon Company

I'm She (yes that's right! My name) and I'm excited to introduce myself to my new followers. I am a Makeup and Hair artist based in London but originally from Abu Dhabi, UAE and Kerala, India. Makeup has been my passion for as long as I can remember. My inspiration for makeup comes from my days as a dance performer and this has definitely influenced my makeup style. I absolutely adore working with my lovely clients, and I feel incredibly blessed and thankful for the opportunity to connect with extraordinary people.

As a makeup artist, I am blessed with the ability to see and appreciate the special kind of beauty that lies within every single person I meet. It's as if I have a sixth sense, where I can envision the stunning results that will come to life once I work my makeup artistry to enhance their natural features. So, if you're a soon-to-be bride, I would love to hear from you! I provide both hair and makeup services and would be thrilled to assist you on your special day.
